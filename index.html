About us

Souljas ATL is a vibrant and dynamic non-profit dedicated to serving the Muslim community in the greater Atlanta area. Founded by a passionate group of young volunteers, our mission is to create spaces for connection, growth, and community engagement. We believe in fostering a sense of belonging and empowerment through events that celebrate our faith, culture, and values.
